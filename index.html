Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer at PetHaven?

At PetHaven, we offer professional pet grooming, a cozy pet hotel for your furry friends, and a wide range of pet supplies, including food, toys, and accessories.

What types of pets can be groomed at PetHaven?

We provide grooming services for various pets, including dogs, cats, and small animals like rabbits and guinea pigs. Our trained staff ensures your pet feels comfortable and cared for.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept various payment methods, including credit cards, bank transfers, and digital wallets.

Is the pet hotel suitable for long-term stays?

Yes, our pet hotel accommodates both short-term and long-term stays. We ensure your pet receives proper care, regular feeding, and exercise throughout their stay.

Are your pet care products eco-friendly?

Many of our pet care products are eco-friendly, including biodegradable waste bags, natural grooming products, and sustainable pet toys. We strive to promote environmentally conscious choices.
